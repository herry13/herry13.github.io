Curriculum Vitae
(06 Aug 2014)

I am a PhD student in the Center of Intelligent Systems and its Applications (CISA) institute, School of Informatics, the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Paul Anderson, Michael Rovatsos, and Gerhard Wickler. My PhD was fully funded by HP Labs Innovation Research Program Award

In my research entitled Automated Planning for Cloud Service Configurations, I apply AI planning techniques to solve problems of System Configuration Management in the cloud computing environment.

I have been selected as the finalist of the UK ICT Pioneers Competition 2014 organised by EPSRC for category Connected World.

From August until December 2013, I was on leave for an internship in HP Labs Bristol, funded by SICSA Postgraduate Industry Internship Programme and HP.

I received the best student paper award at the 25th Large Installation Systems Administration Conference (LISA'11) 2011, for my paper: Automated Planning for Configuration Changes.

I received Master of Science and Bachelor of Science in 2005 and 2002 respectively from Faculty of Computer Science, Universitas Indonesia. In 2002-2003, I was a visiting research student at Imaging Science and Engineering Laboratory, Tokyo Institute of Technology, Tokyo, Japan.
