About

I am a Research Associate at School of Computing Science, University of Glasgow, working on Federated RaspberryPi μ-Infrastructure Testbed (FRμIT).

My works are on Edge Compute and Distributed System.

I received Ph.D. (2014) from the University of Edinburgh, B.Sc. (2002) and M.Sc. (2004) from Universitas Indonesia.
