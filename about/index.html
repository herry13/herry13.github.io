--- title: The color of my history. ---
In 2015, I received a PhD degree in informatics from the University of Edinburgh. Previously, I received BSc and MSc degrees in computer science from Universitas Indonesia. I am currently a research scientist/engineer in Hewlett-Packard Labs in Bristol, United Kingdom, focusing on developing a reliable, trusted, and intelligent system configuration management for large scale distributed system.

achievements

  • PhD full scholarship from HP Labs Innovation Research Program award
  • Finalist (top 5 in category Connected World) of the UK ICT Pioneers Competition 2014 organised by EPSRC UK
  • Internship scholarship from SICSA Postgraduate Industry Internship Programme
  • Best student paper at the 25th Large Installation Systems Administration Conference (LISA'11) 2011, for the paper: Automated Planning for Configuration Changes
